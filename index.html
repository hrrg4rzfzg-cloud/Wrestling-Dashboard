Recovery Rules
Sleep
8–9 hrs/night, non-negotiable at this training volume. This is the biggest lever you control.
Deload
Every 4th week: cut lifting sets ~40%, keep intensity moderate. Let wrestling stay the priority that week.
Daily Mobility
10 min hips, shoulders, neck before or after practice — wrestling stance beats up hip flexors and groin.
Session Spacing
Leave 3+ hrs between lifting and wrestling. Fuel with carbs in between, don't lift on an empty tank.
Full Rest
Sunday off completely. Saturday is optional light pump/mobility, not mandatory.
Overreach Signal
If soreness/fatigue stacks up, cut lifting accessory volume first. Wrestling stays intact — that's the sport.
